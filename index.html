Earthy Foundations

The Essence of Single-Origin Cacao

Our journey begins in the dense micro-climates of small equatorial estates. By partnering directly with local family growers who cultivate heirloom cacao varieties, we secure the finest ethically harvested beans on Earth.

Every crop is treated with profound reverence, preserving natural volcanic nuances and floral complexity that massive industrial processes strip away.

Artisan Mastery

Templed by Hand, Shaped by Time

In our private atelier, we roast in small micro-batches of less than ten kilograms. This gives our master chocolatier full control over flavor profiling, adjusting temperature curves to optimize specific aromatic peaks.

We employ centuries-old stone conching methods, spending up to seventy-two hours developing an ultra-refined, velvety mouthfeel that defies traditional texture.

The Pillars of Falson

Ethically Sourced Cacao

We source exclusively from single-family cooperatives in Madagascar and Venezuela. By paying 40% above fair-trade standard minimums, we foster regenerative farming, preserve volcanic soils, and guarantee the survival of rare heirloom Criollo cocoa pods.

Handcrafted Micro-Batches

Tempered and molded in micro-batches under ten kilograms in our private atelier. Our master chocolatiers utilize traditional granite stone conching wheels for seventy-two consecutive hours, achieving an ultra-refined mouthfeel of less than 15 microns.

Pure Organic Purity

Our formulation pledge is absolute: zero soy lecithin, chemical preservatives, palm fats, or artificial emulsifiers. Crafted only with three natural ingredients—raw cacao beans, raw cocoa butter, and unrefined cane sugar—allowing natural terroir to speak.
